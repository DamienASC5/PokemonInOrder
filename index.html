Pokemon Timeline





Generation 1: Kanto


The Kanto Region is the first region inroduced in Pokemon. It is home to a lot of Pokémon and has a rich history of creating Pokémon with technology.





Generation 2: Johto


The Johto region is the second region introduced into the Pokemon world. Johto and Kanto are part of a large landmass, with everything west of Indigo Plateau, a landmark separating the two, falling in the Johto region.





Generation 3: Hoenn


The Hoenn region is the third entry into the Pokemon world. Hoenn was created after Primal Groudon and Primal Kyogre, two legendary pokemon, were formed. Primal Groudon raised the landmasses and Primal Kyogre filled the seas that would later become Hoenn. It is inspired by the real-world Japanese main island of Kyushu.
